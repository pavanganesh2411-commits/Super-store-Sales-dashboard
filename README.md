 ## Super Store Sales
 Objective: To contribute to sucesss of business by utilizing data analytics techniques, especially focusing on Time series analysis to provide valuable insights.

 Insights:
 -  Most of the payments by cash on delievery followed by online and card.
 -  Most sales are from West Region.
 -   Most sales and profits are in december month.
 -  Consumer segments has most sales.
 -  Office supplies has most sales in category.
 -  Phones has highest sales in subcategory.
 -  Standard class is most prefered shipmode.
